About me.

My name is Jennifer Teagan

I'm 46 years old.

I started sports when I was 6. After 15 years of sports acrobatics at the school of Olympic reserve, I was awarded the title of Master of Sports in this discipline. Already at that time I knew for sure that my life would be connected with sports and a healthy lifestyle.

After graduating in 1997 from the Academy of Physical Education in Montreal and having obtained a diploma in acrobatics and fitness instructor, my career began quickly in the field of sport and fitness.

In 1997, when I was still in Canada, I opened my first fitness gym.

In 2003, our family emigrated to the US and my career continued on a different continent, in a different language, with a different mentality of the people around me and later of the clients. I had to prove again that I knew my job and that I was doing it perfectly.

I had to repeat: universities, schools, courses, master classes, certificates, sports schools, fitness clubs.

There are new friends, acquaintances, teachers, clients...

And at the moment...

  • I'm a personal trainer and fitness instructor for international class CanFitPro.
  • Master of Sports in Sports Acrobatics
  • Coach and choreographer of regional teams in acrobatics and gymnastics
  • Participant and Presenter of International Fitness Conventions (Canada/Germany/US)
  • Presenter and developer of new programs and directions in the field of fitness (Canada)
  • Fitness program participant on TV (Canada)
  • Best Fitness Instructor for Group Programmes 2015/2016 (US)
  • Nominee for the Best Personal Coach of the Year in the category "Sport specific "2016 (Canada)
  • Master Coach of group programs in three fitness clubs (Ottawa)
  • Presenter of Functional Training Master Classes
  • Founder and host of the monthly International Sports Project 30 DAY FITNESS PROGRAM.
  • The author of trainings and techniques for losing extra pounds, thanks to which many girls and women have gotten an athletic body and learned to eat properly.
