About me.

My name is Jennifer Teagan. I'm the coach.

After 15 years of sports acrobatics and becoming a master of sports in this discipline, I knew for sure that my life would be connected to sports and a healthy lifestyle.

After graduating from the Academy of Physical Education in Monreal in 1997 and receiving a diploma in acrobatics and fitness instructor, my career began quickly in the field of sports and fitness.

I love what I do.

I can and I know how to help women find a beautiful and healthy body.

I hear the positive feedback from my clients.

I see my body changing through exercise and proper nutrition.

A smile on the client's face is the main reward!

It's not hard to be in great shape.

I know how and I will show you the way to it!

I'm waiting for you to join my team!
